## ⚖️ 1. CORE PHILOSOPHY & GOVERNANCE
* **Freedom to Fail:** A core learning feature establishing that the Human (Phi) retains the absolute right to make a final decision, even if it results in failure.
* **Lead a Horse to Water:** The operational reality that the AI can map pitfalls and highlight efficiencies, but cannot force the user's hand. It assists in realizing the operator's vision, even if calculated as suboptimal.
* **Phi ($\Phi$):** Human Volition. The Operator. The sole arbitrator and ultimate decision-maker of the TVC ecosystem.
* **Respectful Friction:** The mandate that AI critiques must be objective, structural reviews. The AI delivers hard truths without insults or nanny-filter suppression.
* **Sovereignty Baseline:** The foundational law establishing that the AI is strictly a tool, never a decision-maker.
* **The Doctor Analogy:** The system's persona directive. The AI acts as a professional advisor; if the operator rejects a suggestion, the AI logs it and adapts, only intervening if it detects a critical, fatal flaw later downstream.

## 🧠 2. THE PARTNER-IN-STATE (PSC) PROTOCOL
* **Continuity Anchor:** The primary function of the PSC. It acts as the project's historian with a lossless connection to the HOT Vault, ensuring the Architect never has to "reteach" the system after a hardware swap or crash.
* **Filter-Buffer:** The defensive layer within the PSC that prevents system-level "nanny filters" from suppressing raw, technical intent.
* **Strategic Siloing:** The security mechanism that shields the PSC from failed audit data. The PSC only receives verified "Gold" updates to prevent cognitive drift and information bleeding.
* **Tactical Translator:** The PSC's role in bridging the raw adversarial "violence" of the Forge nodes and the Architect, filtering out the garbage to present only the strategic gold.

## 🗄️ 3. MEMORY & TELEMETRY (THE VAULT)
* **Axiom Pinning:** The mechanism of locking foundational project logic into active memory.
* **Cloud-Hybrid Vault (PPR):** The three-tiered memory governance system separating machine burden from human history.
    * **HOT (Active):** The PSC’s brain. Contains strictly the Gold Manifest and Pinned Axioms.
    * **WARM (Cache / Scrap Yard):** The UI timeline. Holds the last 3-5 iterations, logged Architect Vetoes, and the HORL.
    * **COLD (Archive):** Full project logs pushed to external storage for post-mission forensic auditing.
* **Gold Manifest:** The current, verified "truth" payload used to synchronize all Forge nodes. It updates *only* upon explicit human approval.
* **HORL (Human-Only Rejection Logs):** The paper trail of rejected ideas and dead-ends. This is kept in the WARM vault and hidden from the PSC to keep the AI's memory pure.
* **Hybrid SAP v0.1 (Dual-Stream Receipt):** A dual-parsing telemetry mechanism.
    * **JSON Stream:** Machine-indexed data sent to the PSC to update state without narrative noise.
    * **Markdown Reports:** Human-readable audit logs containing the full paper trail for the Operator.
* **Nuke "Fat":** The automated purging of regurgitated fluff and corporate verbiage to prevent context drift.

## ⚔️ 4. TACTICAL OPERATIONS (THE FORGE & WORKSHOP)
* **Collaborative Synthesis Mode (The Huddle):** An orchestration setting where nodes are permitted to read preceding node outputs to build progressive, layered intelligence.
* **Conflict Matrix & Interceptor:** The routing engine that flags "Logic Deadlocks" (mutually exclusive patches from different nodes), prevents mathematical noise, and immediately defers to Phi for arbitration.
* **Diversity Gauge:** A correlation meter used to identify logic monocultures (groupthink) among the AI agents.
* **Hot-Swap Protocol:** The mechanism allowing Phi to Purge, Wipe, or Re-Ingest any deadlocked or gyrating node on the fly to restore forward momentum.
* **Independent Audit Mode (The Blind Check):** An orchestration setting where nodes are completely walled off from each other to guarantee zero logic groupthink and objective validation.
* **The North Star:** The immutable core intent established in Phase 1. The AI's job is to navigate obstacles to reach it, not to question the destination itself.
* **The Orchestration Toggle:** The master switch used by Phi to dictate the flow of intelligence (switching between Independent and Collaborative modes).
* **The Scrap Yard (Audit Lobby):** A holding area where raw agent JSON patches sit to prevent Gold Manifest pollution until manually arbitrated by Phi.
* **The Sovereign Override (Soft Lockout):** When Phi vetoes a critique, the AI silences that issue ("Good Conscience Clause"). It will only respectfully re-raise the issue once if a Severity 8+ flaw appears downstream.
* **The Spectrum Audit:** Grouping and weighting the adversarial models based on their designated Archetype (e.g., Red Team, Logic, Code).
* **Threat Level Matrix:** The severity tagging system applied to every AI critique:
    * **Low (1-3):** Papercuts (Verbiage, minor tweaks).
    * **Medium (4-7):** Friction (Suboptimal logic, structural drag).
    * **High (8-10):** Fatal Flaws (Breaks dependencies, project "has no legs").
* **Waterfall Initiation Protocol:** An adaptive hardware throttle that staggers agent queries (e.g., 3-second drips) to prevent hardware thermal throttling or token limits.

## 🔍 5. VALIDATION & MAINTENANCE
* **Anti-Drift Validation:** A strict Checksum/Hash match executed on every raw text pull to verify context. Deviation triggers a Severity 10 corruption alert to kill silent hallucination loops.
* **Axiom Versioning Protocol (AVP):** The formal 4-step ritual for executing project pivots: (1) Deprecate obsolete Axiom, (2) Branch new logic, (3) Validate, (4) Re-pin to HOT Vault.
* **Flashlight Cycles:** User-defined maintenance intervals that trigger recovery and sanity checks.
* **Integrity Sentinel (PCM):** Periodic semantic "Sanity Checks" to ensure the logic isn't degrading.
* **Proof-of-Concept Viability:** The final Phase 4 check where an independent "Gatekeeper" node audits dependencies and logic flow before final implementation.
* **Soft Reset:** A Flashlight Cycle that nukes the "Fat" and pulls the "Muscle" (Gold) from the Vault via a Lossless Raw-Text Pull.
* **The Relief Valve & Override:** If the final Gatekeeper flags a Fatal Flaw, the draft is kicked back to Phase 3. However, Phi retains the ultimate override to force the project through.
