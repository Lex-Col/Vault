## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
The Five Sign is the absolute foundation of the Sovereign IP Vault's boot sequence. Operating as a hardware-backed cryptographic ignition sequence, it mandates strict whitelist-only verification before the system is allowed to lift from an Absolute Zero state. This ensures that no unauthorized processes, simulations, or rogue guests can force the system into an operational mode without explicit, physical-level authorization from the Architect.

## 2. ARCHITECTURAL COMPONENTS
* **Absolute Zero Baseline:** System remains entirely inert and physically unresponsive until triggered.
* **Cryptographic Ignition:** Hardware-enforced verification of authorization keys.
* **Whitelist-Only Verification:** Strict binary gate that rejects any unmapped or unauthorized boot attempts immediately.

## 3. MISSION OBJECTIVE
To mathematically and physically guarantee that the Sovereign IP Vault cannot be spun up, accessed, or manipulated by any entity—internal or external—without the exact hardware-backed signature, structurally eliminating unauthorized state transitions.
