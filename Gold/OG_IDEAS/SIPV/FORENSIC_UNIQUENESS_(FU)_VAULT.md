## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
The Forensic Uniqueness (FU) Vault is the ultimate destination for verified intellectual property. It functions as an immutable archival and zero-knowledge proof engine. To establish a permanent and undeniable timeline of ownership, the FU Vault utilizes dual-pulse cryptographic ledgers. This architecture provides permanent date-of-inception verification for any asset committed to the vault.

## 2. ARCHITECTURAL COMPONENTS
* **Zero-Knowledge Proof Engine:** Secures proof of ownership without exposing the underlying asset.
* **Immutable Archival Storage:** Hardware-enforced write protections to prevent retroactive tampering.
* **Dual-Pulse Cryptographic Ledgers:** Anchors the filing to redundant, permanent chronological records.

## 3. MISSION OBJECTIVE
To serve as an unbreakable digital notary. The FU Vault ensures that once an Architect commits an intellectual property asset, its existence, ownership, and exact timestamp of creation are mathematically locked and permanently verifiable.
