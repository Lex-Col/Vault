## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
The Partner-in-State (PSC) Protocol defines the "Partner-in-Crime" relationship between the Architect and a dedicated historian agent. The PSC serves as the project’s continuity anchor, maintaining a lossless connection to the HOT Vault. It is engineered to filter technical "Gold" from narrative "Garbage," ensuring the Architect never has to "reteach" the system or suffer from context drift during long-term missions.

## 2. ARCHITECTURAL COMPONENTS
The PSC operates via three specialized logic layers:
* **Continuity Anchor:** A persistent memory bridge that pulls pinned axioms and Gold Manifests from active storage.
* **Tactical Translator:** A processing layer that bridges the raw, adversarial output of the Forge with the Architect's urban-authentic communication style.
* **Filter-Buffer:** A defensive layer designed to protect raw technical intent from system-level "nanny" filters and narrative suppression.

## 3. MISSION OBJECTIVE
The objective of the PSC is to eliminate "AI Fatigue" and "Context Bleeding." By shielding the PSC from failed audit data and only feeding it verified "Gold" updates, the system maintains a perfect, unpolluted historical record of the project’s evolution.
