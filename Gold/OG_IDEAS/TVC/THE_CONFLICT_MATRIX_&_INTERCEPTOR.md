## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
The Conflict Matrix & Interceptor is a specialized logic-routing mechanism designed to manage "Logic Deadlocks"—situations where two or more adversarial nodes provide diametrically opposed findings. Instead of allowing the system to stall or default to a machine-averaged "middle ground," the Interceptor identifies the contradiction and immediately routes the conflicting data to the Human Sovereign (Phi) for final arbitration.

## 2. ARCHITECTURAL COMPONENTS
* **Conflict Matrix:** A real-time cross-reference of all node outputs to detect contradictory logic patches.
* **The Interceptor:** A high-priority interrupt that halts automated synthesis when a deadlock is detected.
* **Routing Logic:** Bypasses automated processing to present raw, conflicting data directly to the Architect.

## 3. MISSION OBJECTIVE
The objective is to preserve the "Sovereignty of Choice." The Conflict Matrix ensures that critical technical disagreements are never "averaged out" by an algorithm. It forces the system to defer to the Architect's intuition and experience whenever a project reaches a logical crossroads.
