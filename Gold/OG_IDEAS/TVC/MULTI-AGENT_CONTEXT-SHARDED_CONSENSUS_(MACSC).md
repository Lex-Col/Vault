## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
Multi-Agent Context-Sharded Consensus (MACSC) is the foundational architectural framework of The Volition Coalition. It is engineered to replace automated AI bias with an Architect-Governed Forge. By sharding context across multiple adversarial agents and mandating a consensus-based output, the protocol ensures that no single AI model’s logic "monoculture" can dictate the trajectory of a project. It is "Born on the Glass" to provide elite-level strategic ideation on mobile hardware.

## 2. ARCHITECTURAL COMPONENTS
The MACSC framework utilizes three core structural pillars:
* **Context Sharding:** Splitting complex objectives into isolated data packets to prevent cross-contamination of logic between agents.
* **Adversarial Consensus:** Forcing multiple AI nodes to critique and validate each other’s outputs before presentation to Phi.
* **Agnostic Orchestration:** A system-level ability to swap models or architectures without losing the project's logical "North Star."

## 3. MISSION OBJECTIVE
The mission of MACSC is to democratize elite strategic ideation. Its objective is to move the AI from a "decision-maker" to a "high-velocity advisor," ensuring the Human Sovereign (Phi) remains the sole arbitrator. It is designed to provide professional-grade logic hardening for solo operators, regardless of formal discipline.
