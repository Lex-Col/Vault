# PROJECT MANIFEST: THE SCRAP YARD
### Adversarial Isolation Zone & Forensic Paper Trail

**Architect:** Alexander Colclough (@Lex-Col)  
**Version:** v1.0  
**Status:** Proprietary / Intellectual Property

---

## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
The Scrap Yard is the immutable historical paper trail and isolation zone for all rejected logic within The Volition Coalition. It serves as a "Forensic Ledger" that preserves every failed audit, rejected patch, and Architect-vetoed finding. Unlike the PSC (Partner-in-State), which only maintains "Gold" continuity, the Scrap Yard ensures that no technical data is ever truly lost, providing a rich resource for post-mission auditing and "lessons learned" analysis.

## 2. ARCHITECTURAL COMPONENTS
* **Adversarial Isolation:** Rejections are physically sharded from the active Gold Manifest to prevent context pollution.
* **Human-Only Rejection Logs (HORL):** A specific Markdown-based stream available only to the Architect, detailing why specific logic was discarded.
* **Immutable Logging:** A write-only ledger that prevents the AI from "cleaning up" or deleting past failures.

## 3. MISSION OBJECTIVE
The mission of the Scrap Yard is to provide "Total Recall." By preserving the path not taken, the Architect can identify recurring flaws in the AI's logic or revisit previously discarded ideas that may become viable as the project's architecture evolves.
