## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
The Synthesis Equation is the mathematical representation of the logic hardening process within The Volition Coalition. It formalizes the relationship between the Architect (Phi), the Partner-in-State (PSC), and the adversarial output of the Forge nodes (FAN). This equation ensures that the "Hardened Logic" (Lh) is always a result of human arbitration over structured, weighted, and diverse machine intelligence.

## 2. ARCHITECTURAL COMPONENTS
The equation is defined as:
**$$Lh = Phi ( Spsc, CR ( SUM [ FANi * wi * d ] ) )$$**

Variables:
* **Lh:** Hardened Logic Result.
* **Phi:** The Human Sovereign (Sole Arbitrator).
* **Spsc:** The PSC Continuity Anchor/Historian.
* **CR:** Consensus Result of the Forge.
* **FANi:** Individual Forge Agent Node output.
* **wi:** Weight assigned to node i (based on archetype/accuracy).
* **d:** Diversity coefficient (from the Spectrum Audit).

## 3. MISSION OBJECTIVE
The objective is to move from "feeling" that logic is secure to "proving" it mathematically. The equation provides a formal standard for evaluating the quality of synthesis, ensuring that human sovereignty is the multiplier that finalizes all technical data.
