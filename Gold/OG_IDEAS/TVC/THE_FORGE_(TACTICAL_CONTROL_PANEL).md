## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
The Forge is the tactical control panel of The Volition Coalition, governing how independent AI nodes interact. It allows the Architect (Phi) to toggle between isolated auditing and collaborative synthesis. By controlling the "intelligence flow," The Forge prevents logic groupthink and ensures that every technical decision is subjected to a spectrum of adversarial critiques before finalization.

## 2. ARCHITECTURAL COMPONENTS
The Forge operates through three distinct orchestration modes:
* **Independent Audit Mode (The Blind Check):** Nodes are walled off from each other to guarantee zero cross-contamination and objective validation.
* **Collaborative Synthesis Mode (The Huddle):** Nodes are permitted to read preceding outputs to build progressive, layered intelligence.
* **The Spectrum Audit:** A methodology that groups models by Archetype and uses a "Diversity Gauge" to identify logic monocultures.

## 3. MISSION OBJECTIVE
The mission of The Forge is to weaponize adversarial friction. It ensures that no technical flaw is overlooked by forcing models to compete and critique one another. This provides the Architect with a "Logic Hardened" result that has been tested against multiple archetypes of intelligence.
