## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
The Spectrum Audit is the diagnostic engine used within the Forge to identify and neutralize "Logic Monocultures." It groups Federated Agent Nodes (FAN) by their underlying model archetypes and utilizes a "Diversity Gauge" to measure the correlation of their outputs. By ensuring a diverse range of adversarial perspectives, the Spectrum Audit prevents AI "groupthink" and provides a high-fidelity audit of the project's structural integrity.

## 2. ARCHITECTURAL COMPONENTS
* **Diversity Gauge:** A correlation meter used to identify when multiple nodes are providing redundant or biased logic.
* **Threat Level Matrix:** A 1-10 severity scaling system used to tag findings:
    * **Low (1-3):** "Papercuts" (Minor optimizations/formatting).
    * **Medium (4-7):** "Structural Friction" (Logic inconsistencies).
    * **High (8-10):** "Fatal Flaws" (Boundary escapes or mission-critical failures).
* **Archetypal Weighting:** Assigning reliability factors ($wi$) based on the known strengths of specific model architectures.

## 3. MISSION OBJECTIVE
The objective is to provide an objective, non-biased evaluation of technical risk. The Spectrum Audit ensures that a "Fatal Flaw" isn't missed simply because the models used share the same training biases, providing the Architect with a clear, weighted view of the project's health.
