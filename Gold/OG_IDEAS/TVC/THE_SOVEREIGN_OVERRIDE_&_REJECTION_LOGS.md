## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
The Sovereign Override is the primary mechanism for human arbitration within TVC. It codifies the Architect's (Phi) absolute right to veto, modify, or ignore any AI-generated critique. Paired with "Respectful Friction," the protocol ensures that the AI logs rejections and adapts its future output without suppressing its primary mission of adversarial auditing. It maintains a "Human-Only Rejection Log" (HORL) to track rejected logic without polluting the PSC's Gold Manifest.

## 2. ARCHITECTURAL COMPONENTS
* **Soft Lockout:** Silencing a specific AI critique once Phi has vetoed the finding.
* **Respectful Re-raising:** A mandatory protocol where the AI is permitted to re-raise a Severity 8+ flaw only once if it persists in later iterations, before deferring permanently.
* **The Scrap Yard (Audit Lobby):** A holding area where raw patches sit until Phi manually arbitrates their inclusion in the project.

## 3. MISSION OBJECTIVE
The objective is to maintain the "Human Sovereign" baseline. It ensures the AI remains a professional advisor that assists in realizing the operator's vision—even if suboptimal—while ensuring that critical, high-severity flaws are still brought to the Architect's attention in a non-intrusive manner.
