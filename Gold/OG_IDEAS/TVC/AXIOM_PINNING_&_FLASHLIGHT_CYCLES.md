## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
Axiom Pinning is the memory governance protocol of TVC, designed to prevent "Silent Hallucination Loops." It involves pinning foundational project logic to active memory, combined with "Flashlight Cycles"—periodic soft resets that nuke narrative "fat" while pulling "muscle" (Gold) via lossless raw-text retrieval. This ensures that the system state remains anchored to the Architect's original intent, even during high-complexity multi-agent interactions.

## 2. ARCHITECTURAL COMPONENTS
The memory governance system is comprised of four proprietary rituals:
* **Axiom Pinning:** Hard-locking core intent to the agent's primary context window.
* **Soft Reset:** A mandatory purge of conversational noise and non-essential data.
* **Anti-Drift Validation:** A checksum-style verification of current logic against the pinned baseline.
* **Integrity Sentinel (PCM):** Periodic semantic "Sanity Checks" to ensure the AI hasn't deviated from the mission objective.

## 3. MISSION OBJECTIVE
The objective is the total elimination of context drift. By treating AI memory as a volatile resource that must be periodically "scrubbed" and "re-pinned," the Architect ensures that the project trajectory remains 100% accurate over thousands of iterations.
