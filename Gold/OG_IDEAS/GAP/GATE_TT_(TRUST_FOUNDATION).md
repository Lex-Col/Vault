## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
Gate TT is the primary integrity logic gate (0x0316) governing Data Parcel Verification. It acts as the "Notary" of the Triple-Gate Architecture, ensuring that every request passing through the system is signed and validated against the Architect’s root-of-trust. Without a verified cryptographic handshake, the gate remains physically locked, preventing the data from reaching the internal processing registers.

## 2. ARCHITECTURAL COMPONENTS
* **ECDSA-P384 Validation:** High-strength elliptic curve signatures for every data parcel.
* **Root-of-Trust Anchor:** Physical hardware binding to the Architect’s unique cryptographic identity.
* **Integrity Lockdown:** Instantaneous rejection of any parcel that has been modified, tampered with, or incorrectly signed.

## 3. MISSION OBJECTIVE
The objective of Gate TT is to enforce absolute data integrity. It ensures that the AI Guest cannot spoof requests or inject malicious instructions into the system stream. By mandating a hardware-validated signature for every atomic action, Gate TT ensures that only the Architect’s authorized logic is ever executed.
