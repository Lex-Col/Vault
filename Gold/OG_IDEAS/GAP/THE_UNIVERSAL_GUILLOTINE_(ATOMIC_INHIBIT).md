## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
The Universal Guillotine is the definitive hardware kill-sequence for the Guardian Angæl Protocol. It is an atomic inhibit command that bypasses all software layers to communicate directly with the system's Safety-PLC. Upon receiving an HMAC-signed trigger from the Warden, the Guillotine instantaneously cuts physical power to the processing core and initiates a total forensic lockdown of the hardware state.

## 2. ARCHITECTURAL COMPONENTS
* **HMAC-Signed Trigger:** Ensures only the authenticated Warden can initiate the kill-sequence.
* **Atomic Inhibit Signal:** A high-priority hardware interrupt that cannot be masked or delayed.
* **Safety-PLC Integration:** Direct control over the physical power delivery network (PDN).

## 3. MISSION OBJECTIVE
The mission of the Guillotine is to provide a "hard stop" to any boundary escape. It is the final answer to an AI that attempts to exceed its confinement, ensuring that the moment a violation is detected, the system physically ceases to exist in an active state.
