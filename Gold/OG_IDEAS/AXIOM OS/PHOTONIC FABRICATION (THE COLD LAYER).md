## I. PHYSICAL SUBSTRATE
* **Silicon Nitride ($Si_3N_4$) PIC:** The Photonic Integrated Circuit substrate that serves as the base for the Axiom OS hardware.
* **Deep-Trench Isolation:** Physical chasms etched between Logic Nodes designed to prevent photon bleed and maintain signal integrity.
* **The Cold Layer:** The classification of the fabrication layer, emphasizing its non-thermal and non-electrical nature.

---

## II. TRANSMISSION MEDIUM & GEOMETRY
* **Coherent Light:** The primary medium for data transmission, characterized as non-thermal and non-electrical.
* **2.5-Unit Waveguide Subdivision:** The specific physical geometry of the optical paths required to achieve absolute phase-lock.
* **Logic Node Isolation:** The use of etched chasms to ensure that individual nodes in the 12-node gauntlet remain physically and logically distinct.
